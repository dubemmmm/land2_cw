5Land listings — admin upload & the public grid

Two pairs. 5a/5b — the admin form for uploading a plot of land, sharing one draft: a real drag-and-drop photo uploader (reorder by dragging, explicit “set as cover”), Location (required) with an optional free-text Estate field with suggestions, size/use/price/title-document/status controls, and Publish (gated on title + location + a photo) which actually appends the listing. 5c/5d — the public grid, grouped by location, sharing one live filter bar (search, land use, estate, price & size range, location toggles). Publish a listing above and scroll down — it appears in both grids below.
